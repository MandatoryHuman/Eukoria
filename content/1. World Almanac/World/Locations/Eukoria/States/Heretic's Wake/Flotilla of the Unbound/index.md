# Overview
The Flotilla of the Unbound is not a city of stone and earth, but a sprawling, chaotic metropolis of lashed-together galleons, dreadnoughts, and stolen merchant vessels. Serving as the de facto capital of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Heretic's Wake/index]], the Flotilla is constantly on the move through the treacherous northern waters. 

To a newly arriving traveller - assuming they weren't brought aboard as a prisoner  - the Flotilla is a sight of both freedom and danger. The air smells of salt, black powder, and the sharp ozone tang of volatile magic. Because its citizens openly utilize the forbidden [[Traditions of Dismagic]], the armada is frequently surrounded by bizarre environmental side effects, from unnaturally coloured fog to minor reality-warping phenomena.

# Geography & Layout
The "geography" of the Flotilla changes daily. While the core of the city consists of massive, dismasted dreadnoughts chained together into a semi-permanent floating island, the outer rings are completely fluid as pirate crews dock, unlash, and set sail on raids. The Flotilla navigates the harsh, unpredictable environment of Heretic's Wake, a region whose waters still bear the lingering, reality-warping scars of the [[War of Laws]]. The constant, reckless use of Dismagic by the pirates generates immense cosmic friction, making the waters around the Flotilla even more unstable and prone to collateral damage.

# Government & Law
There is no central legal authority in the Flotilla. Instead, the armada is governed by the brutal, decentralized authority of pirate captains. The closest thing to a governing body is the [[Captains' Council]], a shouting match of warlords where authority is maintained only by those with the strongest ships and the most potent command of Dismagic. Laws as the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index]] understand them do not exist here. Disputes are settled through duels, extortion, or the sheer destructive power of clashing Fundamental Essences. Surviving the volatile magical backlash of their own power is simply accepted as the price of living free from canonical law.

# Key NPCs
- [[Captain-Admiral Thorne]] (Level 16 Lich / Runesmith). A terrifyingly calm, undead pirate lord who commands the [[Phylactery Fleet]]. He anchors his soul to his physical flagship, making him nearly impossible to permanently kill as long as his dreadnought remains afloat. He is a devout follower of [[Aethelgard, God of Relics]].
- [[Jeniva The Canvas]] (Level 15 Fleshwarp / Cathartic Mage). A vibrant, deeply empathetic, and physically mutable pirate captain. She is a fierce protector of the Flotilla's vulnerable outcasts and uses Passion-Magic to board enemy ships and incapacitate sailors by hijacking their nervous systems.

# History & Lore
The Flotilla's origins trace back to the direct aftermath of the [[War of Laws]], which concluded 116 years ago. When the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index|Canonical]] gods solidified their rule and outlawed the [[Traditions of Dismagic]], fleeing practitioners, heretics, and outcasts took to the northern waters to escape execution. What began as a desperate, disorganized flight for survival on stolen fishing boats evolved over a century into a formidable, decentralized naval power. Today, the Flotilla is a terrifying armada that holds the orthodox world's trade routes hostage, directly challenging [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Meridia/index]]'s global monopoly.
