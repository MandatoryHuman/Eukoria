# Overview
The Directorate of Vael, located in the central heartlands of [[Cenora]], is a hyper-structured, intellectually terrifying theocracy. It is a nation where the law is absolute, and privacy is considered a moral failing. Guarded by impenetrable psychic wards and governed by literal hive-minds, Vael boasts a crime rate of virtually zero - after all, the state's inquisitors know a crime is going to happen before the perpetrator even commits to the idea.

# Geography & Climate
Vael is characterized by orderly, mathematically planned cities set amidst temperate plains and meticulously cultivated farmlands. The borders are not marked by walls, but by invisible, towering psychic barriers - manifestations of [[Traditions of Magic#Occult Magic (Spirit + Mind)|Occult]] Magic - that immediately alert the authorities to any unauthorized crossing or hostile intent. The weather itself seems subdued here, heavily regulated by state-sponsored weather-mages to ensure perfectly predictable crop yields.

# Society & Culture
Society in Vael is built on radical transparency and intellectual contribution. Citizens are expected to be rigidly honest and orderly, living by the exact letter and spirit of the law as dictated by [[Solon, God of Law]]. The pursuit of knowledge is paramount, honouring [[Oclera, God of Knowledge]]. Occult Magic (the blend of Spirit and Mind) is the most revered tradition; bards and psychics hold high status, serving as living lie-detectors, judges, and city planners. The concept of "[[Traditions of Dismagic|Dismagic]]" is treated with such revulsion that simply thinking about it too loudly will trigger a psychic audit.

Because privacy is culturally equated with deception and criminal intent, Vaelian architecture reflects this transparency. Homes are built with sheer glass walls and often lack interior doors. To cope with the immense psychological pressure of constant surveillance, citizens practice "The Blanking" - a state-sanctioned, daily meditation technique designed to completely empty the mind of all thought. This allows them to relax and sleep without accidentally triggering an inquisitor's ward with a stray, rebellious dream.

# Government & Politics
Vael is governed by the Panopticon Council, a body of elite Occult mages and high clerics. Below them are the Truth-Seekers, psychic inquisitors who police the populace. Justice is swift and lacks traditional trials; a psychic simply reads the accused's mind, and judgement is passed. Punishments usually involve having the criminal intent surgically removed from the offender's consciousness, a process that often leaves them docile and emotionless.

# Diplomatic Relations
- [[Meridia]] (Cold/Hostile): Vael views Meridia and [[High Arbiter Garadwen]] with utter disgust. To the Directorate, Meridia's use of loopholes and their worship of [[Lethos, God of Trickery]] is a profound mockery of true law and order.
- [[The Daeshin Null-State]] (Active Containment): Vael devotes a massive amount of its military and psychic resources to maintaining a hard quarantine on its eastern border to prevent the madness of Daeshin from bleeding into their perfect society.

# History & Lore
The foundations of Vael were laid in the immediate aftermath of the [[War of Laws]]. When [[Lethos, God of Trickery|Lethos]] assassinated [[Syla, Goddess of Records]], the world's shared history was shattered. Refusing to let civilization descend into amnesia and chaos, surviving clerics of Solon allied with the newly empowered followers of Oclera. Together, they vowed to build a society where truth could never be erased or subverted again, sacrificing individual privacy for collective, unbreakable order.

# Notable Locations
- [[Axiom's Hold]]: The capital city, built in a perfect geometric grid. At its center lies a massive spire of white stone where the Panopticon Council resides, projecting their psychic surveillance across the nation.
- [[The Glass Monasteries]]: High-altitude retreats where devoted clerics of Oclera physically link their consciousnesses together into massive hive-mind think tanks, processing Eukoria's most complex magical and societal theorems.

# Prominent Factions
- The Silent Chorus: The state-sponsored hive-mind of Ocleran clerics. They process intelligence gathered by Vaelian spies across the world.
- The Astral Wardens: Elite Occult knights devoted to Solon who protect the nation against psychic intrusion and sever the astral tethers of invading Dismagic practitioners.
