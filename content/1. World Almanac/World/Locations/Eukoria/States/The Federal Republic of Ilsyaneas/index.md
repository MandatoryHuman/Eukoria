# Overview

Situated on the western shores of [[Cenora]], the Federal Republic of Ilsyaneas is an ideological anomaly in a world dominated by absolute monarchs and divine theocracies. It is a society striving for self-determination and civic equality, explicitly rejecting the absolute mandates of the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index|Canonical Gods]]. By democratising power through advanced innovation and strict secular governance, Ilsyaneas stands as a beacon of progressive engineering and a fierce rival to the world's established autocracies.

# Geography & Climate

The Republic enjoys a favourable, temperate-to-warm climate. The geography is defined by rolling, fertile plains that abruptly thrust upward into a massive, stark central feature known as Mount Solace - a colossal, snow-capped caldera. The sheer cliffs and mountainous borders of the region provide impenetrable defensive terrain against foreign incursions, allowing the Republic to safely cultivate its industrial heartlands.

# Society & Culture

Ilsyan culture is defined by robust civic engagement, fierce public debate, and a profound respect for engineering and arcane theory. Built upon the [[Fundamental Essences]] of Mind and Matter, the citizens apply these forces to democratise power. They rely on reliable, mortal-made machinery and rudimentary steam-powered transit systems rather than divine miracles. The populace maintains a high standard of living, and every citizen is expected to serve in a highly disciplined militia to defend the Republic's progressive ideals.

# Government & Politics

The government operates under a complex system of representative democracy. A bicameral legislature dictates the law: a lower house composed of elected representatives from every district and guild, and an upper Senate of magistrates who oversee constitutional law. The head of state is an elected Chancellor who serves a strictly limited term, effectively preventing the rise of a new autocrat.

- [[1. # History & Lore

Following the devastation of the [[War of Laws]], the founders of Ilsyaneas rejected the notion that mortal destiny should be dictated by the whims of warring monoliths. Viewing the Canonical Gods merely as powerful natural forces rather than moral authorities, they banded together to build a society where power was held by the citizenry. Utilising the immense geothermal and Primal energies of the dormant Mount Solace, they forged an industrial revolution that secured their independence and transformed them into a major geopolitical power.

# Notable Locations

- [[The Caldera Forum]]: The sprawling, industrialized capital built directly into the slopes and geothermal vents of Mount Solace.
- Mount Solace: The colossal dormant supervolcano that serves as the industrial and spiritual heart of the Republic.
