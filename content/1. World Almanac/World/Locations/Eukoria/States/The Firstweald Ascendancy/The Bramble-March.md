# Overview

The Bramble-March is a highly volatile, magically engineered perimeter of deadly flora and fauna that aggressively guards the eastern and southern land borders of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Firstweald Ascendancy/index|The Firstweald Ascendancy]]. To outsiders, it looks like an impenetrable wall of towering, thorn-choked trees and writhing vines. It is not a natural forest, but a biological weapon designed to forcefully repel the industrialized machinery of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Meridia/index|Meridia]] and the entropic ash of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Kazarn Hegemony/index|The Kazarn Hegemony]].

# Ecology & Environment

The ecology of the Bramble-March is hyper-accelerated by Primal Magic. The flora here grows at an alarming, visible rate. If a path is hacked through the brush with machetes, the vines will literally slither and weave themselves back together within an hour. The environment is heavily biokinetically engineered: plants have been bred to react violently to the scent of refined iron, the residue of Arcane magic, or the smell of burning coal.

# Hazards & Encounters

The Bramble-March actively hunts trespassers who lack the blessing of the Firstweald druids.

- Creatures:
  - Awakened Trees
  - Flytraps
  - Engineered dire beasts (like armour-plated bears and venomous panthers)
  - Fey border-patrols.
- Environmental Hazards:
  - Razor-Vine Thickets: Vines that lie dormant until a creature carrying refined metal walks past, at which point they snap shut like steel traps, inflicting severe slashing and bleed damage.
  - Spore-Clouds: Pockets of hallucinogenic pollen that cause outsiders to lose their sense of direction, wander in circles, and eventually succumb to exhaustion, becoming fertilizer for the roots.

# Landmarks & Points of Interest

- The Weeping Watchtowers: Massive, living oak trees that have been coaxed into the shapes of towering sentinels. Druids use them as outposts and invoke the trees to "weep" a highly acidic sap onto unauthorized caravans passing below.

# Natural Resources

The Bramble-March is a treasure trove of incredibly potent Primal reagents. Rare venoms, medicinal saps, and "Living-Lumber"  - wood that continues to heal itself even after being cut and shaped - are highly sought after by smugglers brave enough to risk the harvest.

# Myths & Lore

The March was rapidly cultivated 116 years ago during the climax of the [[War of Laws]]. As the divine collateral damage threatened to engulf the Firstweald, the ancestors of the [[The Circle of the Root]] channelled unprecedented amounts of Primal Magic. They forced a 200km stretch of forest to evolve and weaponize itself in a matter of days and successfully protected their sacred cradle from the grinding destruction that ravaged Eukoria most of.
