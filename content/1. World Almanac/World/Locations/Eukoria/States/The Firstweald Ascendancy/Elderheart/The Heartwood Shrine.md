The central temple of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Firstweald Ascendancy/Elderheart/index|Elderheart]] dedicated to [[Kurnos, God of Nature]] and [[Maelis, Goddess of Fertility and Harvest]]. Located in a massive, naturally hollowed chamber in the trunk, the walls pulse with the tree's glowing sap. It is here that [[1. World Almanac/World/NPCs/Chosen Ones/index|Chosen Ones]] receive their boons of biokinesis and vitality.
