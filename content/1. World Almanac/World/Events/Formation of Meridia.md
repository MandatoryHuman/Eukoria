The very same year the [[War of Laws]] ended, a young [[High Arbiter Garadwen]] - along with a number of now deceased allies, began the task of finding a safe route through the [[God-Scar Strait]]. Within two years they began offering their services to the still recovering nations of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/index]], shipping men and cargo from one continent to the other - though their success rate was considerably worse than it is today. By the end of the year, the money they had earned started to be put towards building their company camp into something more substantial; a settlement that would eventually become [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Meridia/Obolus/index]], capital of of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Meridia/index]].
