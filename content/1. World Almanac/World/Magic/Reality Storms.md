Reality Storms are terrifying, unpredictable environmental hazards that plague specific, heavily scarred regions of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/index]]. They are not meteorological events driven by wind or rain, but rather violent, invisible waves of cosmic friction. They occur where the fabric of reality was permanently torn during the [[War of Laws]], or where the [[Basic Essences]] of Presence and Absence are forced into direct, clashing contact.

To those caught inside a Reality Storm, the laws of physics, time, and biology cease to function correctly. Gravity may stutter, memories can be ripped from the mind, and matter may spontaneously transmute. 

# Regional Manifestations
Because these storms are tied to the specific divine fallout of their respective regions, they manifest in wildly different, horrific ways across the continents.

## The [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Daeshin Null-State/index|Daeshin]] Border of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Glandaric Marches/index]]
The storms that bleed out of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Daeshin Null-State/index]] into the Marches are perhaps the most psychologically devastating on the continent. Born from the explosive death of [[Thrum, God of Madness]], these storms manifest as silent, invisible shockwaves of pure Absence crashing against the physical world.
- Memory Erasure: Unprotected individuals caught in the open risk having their personal histories, names, and loyalties permanently scrubbed from their minds. This is why Glandaric citizens rely so heavily on Occult tattoos and emotional "Anchors" to ground their souls to their bodies.
- The Unquiet Dead: Because the void abhors the rampant vitality of the living, these storms frequently cause the sudden, violent resurrection of the dead. Pure Presence forcefully intrudes on the vacuum, causing long-buried corpses and recent battlefield casualties to rise from the grave as twisted, reality-warped monstrosities that attack anything with a beating heart.

## The [[God-Scar Strait]]
The body of water dividing [[Antoria]] and [[Cenora]] was ground zero for immense divine collateral damage. The Reality Storms here distort the physical world, making it the most treacherous shipping lane in existence.
- Spontaneous Whirlpools: Without warning, the ocean can fold in on itself, creating massive whirlpools that instantly suck down anything caught within them.
- Tidal Inversions: The storms frequently defy the natural laws of [[Vellora, Goddess of Sea and Storms]], causing rain to fall upwards or the ocean surface to temporarily harden into a substance akin to jagged glass, shattering the hulls of ships unwise enough to attempt to run [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Meridia/index]]'s blockades.

## [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Heretic's Wake/index]]
The northern waters are already unstable, but the constant, reckless use of the [[Traditions of Dismagic]] by the [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Heretic's Wake/Flotilla of the Unbound/index]] generates immense cosmic friction that actively feeds the local storms.
- Arcane Mutations: A storm rolling through the Wake often resembles a neon, kaleidoscopic fog. Getting caught in it can trigger forced evolutionary leaps tied to [[Lysia Bind-breaker, Goddess of Passion]]. Sailors might temporarily sprout extra appendages, lose their sight to gain telepathy, or have their ships' timber spontaneously sprout writhing, fleshy tentacles.
- Wild Magic Surges: Spells cast within these storms are almost guaranteed to misfire, amplifying their effects tenfold or completely altering their intended [[Fundamental Essences]].

## The [[Rust-Wastes]] of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Kazarn Hegemony/index|Kazarn]]
In the centre of [[Antoria]], the Reality Storms align with the domain of [[Xerith, God of Erosion]].
- Entropic Squalls: These storms look like suffocating clouds of grey ash. Within the squall, the natural decay of the universe is hyper-accelerated. A freshly forged iron sword will rust to useless flakes in a matter of seconds, and a living creature's bones will become brittle and crack under the pressure of their own muscles. [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Kazarn Hegemony/index]]'s soldiers are forced to constantly cycle out their equipment and retreat when the ash clouds gather.

# Survival and Wards
Surviving a Reality Storm generally requires immense preparation. Orthodox nations like [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Directorate of Vael/index]] utilise massive, city-spanning Occult wards to create "blank spaces" that repel the cosmic friction.

For those forced to survive in the wilds or the brutal frontiers like [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/The Glandaric Marches/index]], survival is visceral. Physical tethers, such as ritually scarring one's own skin or holding onto emotionally charged "Anchors," help tether citizens, preventing the storm from scrubbing the victim from existence.
