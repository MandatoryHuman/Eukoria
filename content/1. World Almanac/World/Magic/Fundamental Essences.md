The theology and physical reality of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/index|Eukoria]] are inextricably linked to the cosmic geometry of the four Fundamental Essence. Unlike the absolute binary of the [[Basic Essences]], the Fundamentals are the building blocks of the mortal condition, orthodox magic, and the material plane.
Every mortal soul is a complex blend of all four, and the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/Gods of Magic/index|Gods of Magic]] are each comprised of a pairing of two adjacent Fundamental Essences on the cosmic wheel.

# The Four Fundamentals

## Mind

The essence which allows beings to think, rationalise, and remember. It is the seat of intellect, logic, and consciousness, separating sentient beings from unthinking beasts or inanimate objects. When combined with other essences, it forms the basis of intellectual and psychic magics.

## Spirit

The essence which makes up the soul and spiritual being of all creatures. It is the intangible tether that connects the mortal plane to the divine and the afterlife, representing willpower, faith, and the eternal self.

## Life

_Also known as "Vital Essence."_ The essence that animates reality and fuels a being's instinctual behaviours. It governs the beating of the heart, the growth of the wild, and the raw biological drive to survive. Notably, Life also encompasses the animating force that fuels unlife.

## Matter

The essence which defines the physical components and reactions that composite reality. It is the tangible substance of the universe - the earth, the elements, and the strict physical laws that govern them.
