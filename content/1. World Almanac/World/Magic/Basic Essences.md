While the four [[Fundamental Essences]] make up the complex reality of the mortal plane and the orthodox magic system, the Basic Essences serve as the absolute, binary anchors of existence.
They are the most primordial forces in the cosmos. [[The Twins]] - the primordial deities of Eukora - are solely comprised of a single Basic Essence each. When a Basic Essence interacts with a Fundamental, it creates the broader domains of the [[Canonical Gods]] outside of the Gods of Magic.

# The Two Basic Essences

## Presence
The concept of something being created, existing, and reproducing. It is the unmitigated force of manifestation, growth, and the addition of new elements into reality. Pure Presence is too overwhelming for physical reality to sustain for long without the tempering of the Fundamentals.

## Absence
The concept of something being destroyed, decaying, and going extinct. It is the inevitable force of the void, erasure, and the subtraction of elements from reality. Pure Absence is a terrifying, consuming vacuum that reality naturally abhors.

## The Paradox of Fate
Combining Presence and Absence is a cosmic contradiction. The only known entity comprised of both Basic Essences is [[Aporia, God of Fate]], a deeply unnatural and taboo Greater God who embodies the boundary between what is and what is not.
