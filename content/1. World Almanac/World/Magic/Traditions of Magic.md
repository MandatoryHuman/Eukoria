> [!quote|Author] Primer on Eukorian Thaumaturgy 
> "Magic is not a chaotic force to be tamed. It is a precise equation written in the language of the four Essences. Follow the wheel, and the universe will provide."

In Eukoria, orthodox spellcasting is divided into four distinct **Traditions of Magic**. These traditions are entirely dependent on the cosmic wheel of the four Fundamental Essences. A tradition is formed when two adjacent Essences are channeled in perfect harmony.

Because these pairs naturally flow into one another, they create stable, predictable phenomena that do not inherently damage the fabric of reality or the caster's soul. These four pillars are governed by the pact-bound [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/Gods of Magic/index|Gods of Magic]].

# Divine Magic (Life + Spirit)
Governed by [[Amnis the Burdened, Goddess of Forgiveness|Amnis, the Burdened]], Divine Magic is the harmonious blend of physical vitality and the ethereal soul.

## Focus
Healing, spiritual intervention, and the cycle of mortality.

## Practice
Worshippers, clerics, and Oracles channel this blend to mend flesh through spiritual intervention, commune with spirits, and safeguard the living. It can be used as a shield to protect a soul from leaving the mortal coil, or as a scythe to hasten its departure.

# Occult Magic (Spirit + Mind)
Governed by [[Solon, God of Law]], Occult Magic is the cerebral blend of the intangible soul and pure consciousness.

## Focus
Psychic phenomena, astral projection, and the defence of the mind.

## Practice
Practitioners of this tradition, such as bards and psychics, unravel psychic mysteries and construct impenetrable wards against mental intrusion. Masters of the Occult can safely untether their consciousness from their physical form to walk the astral planes - or violently sever the astral tethers of their enemies.

# Arcane Magic (Mind + Matter)
Governed by [[Aureon, God of Commerce and Industry]], Arcane Magic is the calculated blend of intellect and physical substance.

## Focus 
Transmutation, telekinesis, and artifice.

## Practice
This is the tradition most favoured by traditional Wizards and Magi. They manipulate physical reality through pure, calculated thought, allowing them to transmute base elements and construct miraculous wonders of artifice. Conversely, this same logic can be weaponized to mathematically deconstruct the works of their enemies into dust.

# Primal Magic (Matter + Life)
Governed by [[Kurnos, God of Nature]], Primal Magic is the visceral blend of physical substance and biological vitality.

## Focus
Shapeshifting, biokinesis, and environmental control.

## Practice
Druids, Rangers, and Fey channel this visceral blend to command the raw strength of nature and mould the physical forms of living beasts. It is deeply tied to the environment, allowing casters to cultivate flourishing new ecosystems, or violently reclaim civilized lands for the earth.
