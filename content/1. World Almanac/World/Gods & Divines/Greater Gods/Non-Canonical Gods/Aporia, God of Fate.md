Aporia is the Greater god of Fate and Destiny. They are a composite of the Basic Essences of Presence and Absence. As these elements are so disparate, they fail to fully combine into one being leaving Aporia as a multitude of personalities and appearances which meld into each other unpredictably. While this temperamentality - alongside their non-canonical status - doesn't lend Aporia to having a very large following, those who do worship them have developed a number of special rituals that allow them to evoke a particular aspect of the god as they need.
As a god, Aporia acts as an arbiter of what is (Presence) versus what could have been (Absence), weaving the threads of time, knowing both the events that manifest in reality, and those infinite possibilities that are cut away into nothingness.

## Divine Intercession

Aporia occasionally blesses those who embrace their destiny or make difficult choices that preserve the fabric of reality. Conversely, they curse those who arrogantly attempt to rewrite what was or cheat the fate assigned to them.

[Minor Boon::You gain a fleeting glimpse into the immediate future, allowing you to choose the best path. Once per day, you can cast sure strike as an innate occult spell.]
[Moderate Boon::Aporia allows you to briefly slip into an alternate reality where a blow never landed. Once per day, when you are targeted by a physical attack, you can use your reaction to become temporarily concealed against that attack. If the flat check fails, the attack misses you entirely as you phase into Absence.]
[Major Boon::The ultimate weaver favours you. After a creature you can see takes an action that involves rolling one or more dice (for example a skill check or saving throw), after seeing the results of the action you can choose to follow a different timeline. Time rewinds to the moment before that roll was made and you can choose the exact result of each dice roll involved in that action from within each die's usual bounds. Once used, Aporia forgets you, and this boon is lost permanently.]

[Minor Curse::You are burdened by a paralyzing awareness of all possible outcomes of a situation. You suffer a permanent -2 status penalty to Initiative rolls as you struggle to choose which reality to act upon.]
[Moderate Curse::You are haunted by what could have been. An effect causes you to reroll a check, you take a -4 status penalty to the re-roll.]
[Major Curse::The very fates are working against you. You cannot benefit from Fortune effects and any time one of your checks, saves, or attack rolls are effected by a misfortune effect, you become off-guard for a minute.]
