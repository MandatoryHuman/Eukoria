> [!quote] "Do not pray to the monoliths for mercy. You might as well beg the hurricane to alter its course, or ask gravity to forgive your fall."

The Greater Gods of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/index]] are cosmic monoliths, immense and unyielding entities that form the absolute structural pillars of reality. Unlike the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Lesser Gods/index]], who possess a mortal-like complexity, Greater Gods are fundamentally alien in their psychology.

# Cosmic Geometry
A Greater God is defined strictly: they are comprised of **exactly two** Essences. Because they lack the complete, balanced spectrum of the four Fundamental Essences (Life, Spirit, Mind, Matter) found in mortal souls and Lesser Gods, they often act in inscrutable and unpredictable ways - at least by mortal standards.
There are two groups of Greater Gods, divided in orthodox and unorthodox categories based on the relationship between their essences:
- **[[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index]]**: Formed from two _adjacent_ Essences. Harmonious and stable.
- **[[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Non-Canonical Gods/index]]**: Formed from two _non-adjacent_ Essences. More raw power, but less control.

# [[Aporia, God of Fate]]
While most Greater Gods mix Fundamental Essences (or a Fundamental with a Basic Essence), there is one exception: Aporia. Born from the climax of the ancient _War of Laws_, Aporia is the paradoxical combination of the two Basic Essences: Presence and Absence. Aporia embodies the boundary between what is and what is not. Because manifesting a void is a cosmic contradiction, many worshippers of orthodox deities across the entire spectrum of morality view Aporia as deeply unnatural and taboo.

# Divine Conduits - The [[1. World Almanac/World/NPCs/Chosen Ones/index]]
Because Greater Gods are too vast and specialized to easily parse the nuance of the mortal plane, they interact with Eukoria primarily through Chosen Ones. These rare mortals are selected to act as living, physical conduits for the deity's will.
In exchange for acting as the god's hands and voice, a Chosen One is granted a fraction of their patron's power.
The abilities granted to a Chosen One are extreme and hyper-focused, dictated strictly by the patron's two Essences (ranging from subtle, overwhelming psychic dominion to earth-shattering transmutations of matter).
