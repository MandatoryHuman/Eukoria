Bella is the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/index|Greater God]] of War, martial glory, and unwavering willpower. In the cosmic geometry of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/index|Eukoria]], she represents the harmonious blend of the [[Fundamental Essences|Fundamental Essence of Spirit]] - the intangible tether of willpower and faith - and the [[Basic Essences|Basic Essence of Presence]] - the unmitigated force of manifestation. She is the physical manifestation of courage and the literal embodiment of the fighting spirit.

![[Assets/Gods/Bella.webp|400]]

As a prominent figure among the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index|Canonical Gods]], Bella’s domain over conflict is highly structured and predictable. She does not revel in senseless slaughter or chaotic bloodlust; rather, she governs the disciplined march of armies, the sanctity of an honourable duel, and the martial codes that elevate soldiers above mere beasts. Because she favours direct, honourable conflict over subterfuge, she is a staunch ideological enemy of [[Lethos, God of Trickery]].

Her worshippers are predominantly soldiers, paladins, marshals, and honourable mercenaries. To follow Bella is to believe that the ultimate test of a mortal's Spirit is manifested on the battlefield. Her [[1. World Almanac/World/NPCs/Chosen Ones/index|Chosen Ones]] are living banners of morale, tasked with leading from the front and physically manifesting their divine willpower to turn the tide of impossible battles.

## [Divine Intercession](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=804)

Bella grants her boons to those who display unimaginable bravery in the face of certain defeat, and those who uphold the strict codes of honourable combat. She turns her wrath upon cowards, deserters, and those who rely on deceit to win their battles.

\[Minor Boon::Bella bolsters your fighting spirit, making you fearless. You gain a +1 status bonus to saving throws against fear effects. If you roll a success on a saving throw against a fear effect, you get a critical success instead.]
\[Moderate Boon::Your weapons become conduits for Bella's radiant presence. Once per day, you can cast Heroism on yourself as an innate divine spell. While this spell is active, your weapon Strikes deal an additional 1d6 spirit damage.]
\[Major Boon::Bella allows you to manifest your Spirit to protect an honourable ally. Once per day, when an adjacent ally is hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to redirect all the damage and effects of that attack to yourself. You gain resistance to all damage equal to your level against this attack.]

\[Minor Curse::The weight of your cowardice physically drags you down. You take a permanent -10 foot status penalty to your Speed whenever you are moving away from an enemy.]
\[Moderate Curse::Your Spirit is fragile and easily broken. Whenever an ally within 30 feet of you is reduced to 0 Hit Points, you automatically become Frightened 2. You cannot reduce this condition below 1 until that ally is stabilized or healed.]
\[Major Curse::Because you refuse to stand as a beacon, Bella strips you of your Presence entirely. You become easily forgotten by your allies in the heat of battle; they cannot target you with beneficial spells, healing, or Aid.]
