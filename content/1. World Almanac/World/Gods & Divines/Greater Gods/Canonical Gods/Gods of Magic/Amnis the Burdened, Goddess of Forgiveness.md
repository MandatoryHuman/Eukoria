Amnis is the Greater Goddess of Forgiveness and Redemption, and the Goddess of Divine Magic.

![[Assets/Gods/Amnis, The Burdened.webp|400]]

Amnis teaches mercy above all. Her capacity for forgiveness is so profound that if a transgressor is truly remorseful, Amnis will absorb the sin directly from their soul. She carries this dark burden herself, granting the mortal true "amnesty" - her name even being the very root of the word.

Legends claim that because of the sheer volume of sin she contains, her death would release a shockwave of unholy energy capable of obliterating the world. While mortal scholars debate the truth of this theory, it is a historical fact that during the [[War of Laws]], not a single deity dared to make an attempt on her life.

The followers of Amnis are staunch pacifists. Her [[1. World Almanac/World/NPCs/Chosen Ones/index|Chosen Ones]] are strictly forbidden from killing or lying, tasked instead with spreading her message through diplomacy and grace. To aid them, Amnis provides her Champions with no offensive magic, instead granting them two miraculous gifts. The first is universal communion - the ability to speak with and understand any intelligent lifeform. The second is a conditional immortality; should a Chosen fall while pursuing their peaceful duties - a frequent hazard for unarmed diplomats - they are able to return to life, resurrecting at a sanctuary of their choosing.

## [Divine Intercession](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=804)

Amnis often gives boons to those who make a sacrifice to do better or take a risk to redeem another. She typically curses those who make use of another creatures mercy for their own personal gain.

\[Minor Boon::You are able to disable enemies without killing them. You do not take the usual -2 circumstance penalty when making a non-lethal attack with a lethal weapon.]
\[Moderate Boon::You are able to communicate effectively with almost all intelligent life. As long as your target knows at least one language, you are able to speak to them and use actions on them as though you knew their language.]
\[Major Boon::In exchange for risking your life for the cause of forgiveness, Amnis offers you an iota of protection. The first time you die after receiving this boon, if you were killed by a creature you had not directly dealt damage to you are resurrected at dawn in a location of your choosing that you have visited before. You then lose this boon, whether or not it activated (It may be granted again).]

\[Minor Curse::It seems a little more difficult for you to get that final blow in. Every weapon you wield is treated as though it has the [Nonlethal](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=661) trait.]
\[Moderate Curse::You are stripped of your ability to deceive those who show you grace. You permanently take a -4 circumstance penalty to checks to Lie.]
\[Major Curse::You are completely physically incapable of taking a life. If an attack or spell you use would reduce a creature to 0 or fewer Hit Points, the creature is instead reduced to 1 Hit Point, and you take Spirit damage equal to the amount that was prevented.]
