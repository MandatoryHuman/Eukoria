Maelis is the [[Greater Gods|Greater God]] of Fertility, the harvest, and explosive biological growth. In the cosmic geometry of [[Eukoria]], she represents the harmonious, [[Canonical Gods|Canonical]] intersection of the [[Fundamental Essences|Fundamental Essence of Life]] and the [[Basic Essences|Basic Essence of Presence]].

![[Maelis.webp|400]]

The [[Fundamental Essences|Essence of Life]] governs the beating of the heart, the growth of the wild, and the raw biological drive to survive. The [[Basic Essences|Essence of Presence]] is the unmitigated force of manifestation, growth, and the addition of new elements into reality. When combined smoothly as a [[Canonical Gods|Canonical God]], Maelis embodies the physical manifestation of spring - the birthing of new generations, the blooming of flora, and the bountiful, life-sustaining harvest.

Unlike the  unnatural, forced evolutionary leaps practiced by followers of the [[Traditions of Dismagic|Dismagic]] Goddess [[Lysia Bind-breaker, Goddess of Passion|Lysia]], Maelis governs natural, orthodox reproduction and growth. Her worshippers are predominantly farmers, midwives, parents, and community leaders. To follow Maelis is to believe that the ultimate expression of the divine is the creation and nurturing of new life.

Her [[Chosen Ones|Chosen Ones]] act as avatars of her will on Eukora , tasked with protecting the vulnerable young, ensuring communities survive harsh winters with bountiful crops, and cultivating life in the barren, warped landscapes left behind by the [[War of Laws|War of Laws]].

## [Divine Intercession](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=804)
Maelis rewards those who foster growth, protect the vulnerable, and share their bounty. She aggressively starves out those who salt the earth, abandon the young, or selfishly hoard the harvest while others wither.

[Minor Boon::Maelis blesses your hands with the spark of vitality. You gain a +1 status bonus to Medicine checks to Administer First Aid or Treat Disease. Additionally, any natural food or water you gather is always perfectly safe to consume and highly nutritious.]
[Moderate Boon::The unmitigated force of Presence amplifies your biological drive. Whenever you receive magical healing, you regain an additional 1d8 Hit Points. Furthermore, you automatically succeed at the flat check to remove persistent bleed damage.]
[Major Boon::Maelis grants you the ultimate authority of creation. Once, when you or an ally within 30 feet would be reduced to 0 hit points, the target is instead instantly restored to their maximum Hit Points. Simultaneously, a burst of rampant, flourishing flora erupts in a 30-foot radius, creating difficult terrain that only impedes your enemies. Once used, Maelis's focus shifts elsewhere, and this boon is permanently lost.]
  
[Minor Curse::The spark of Life naturally recoils from you. Natural food spoils rapidly in your inventory, turning to rot within hours. Furthermore, you take a permanent -2 status penalty to Medicine checks.]
[Moderate Curse::Maelis drains the biological drive from your physical form. Your body struggles to naturally recover; you only regain half the normal amount of Hit Points from resting, and whenever you gain the Drained condition, its value is automatically increased by 1.]
[Major Curse::The Goddess completely strips the Essence of Life and Presence from your physical form, rendering you a barren husk. You permanently gain the Drained 2* and Enfeebled 2 conditions, which cannot be removed by any mortal magic. Furthermore, any non-magical plant life you step on instantly withers into dry ash.]
