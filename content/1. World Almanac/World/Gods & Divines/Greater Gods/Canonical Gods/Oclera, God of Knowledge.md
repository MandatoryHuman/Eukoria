Oclera is the [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/index|Greater God]] of Knowledge, truth, and enlightenment. As a cosmic monolith comprised of exactly two Essences, they represent the harmonious, [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index|Canonical]] intersection of the [[Fundamental Essences|Fundamental Essence of Mind]] and the [[Basic Essences|Basic Essence of Presence]].

![[Assets/Gods/Oclera.webp]]

The [[Fundamental Essences|Essence of Mind]] is the seat of intellect, logic, consciousness, and the ability to remember. The [[Basic Essences|Essence of Presence]] is the unmitigated force of manifestation, growth, and the addition of new elements into reality. When combined as a [[1. World Almanac/World/Gods & Divines/Greater Gods/Canonical Gods/index|Canonical God]], Oclera embodies the physical manifestation of thought - the spark of a new idea, the uncovering of a hidden truth, and the active, collaborative expansion of mortal intellect.

Oclera's current role in the pantheon was deeply shaped by the tragic loss of [[Syla, Goddess of Records]]. Her death during the [[War of Laws]] caused great chaos as the shared consciousness her clerics possessed had been relied upon to store much of the history of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/index|Eukoria]]. Refusing to let such a profound intellectual tool die with her, Oclera spent years meticulously researching and reverse-engineering the remnants of Syla's shared-consciousness magic.

However, where Syla used this psychic network passively to store records, Oclera applied the technique to actively process information. Today, Oclera's most devoted clerics willingly link their consciousnesses together to form vast, hyper-intelligent hive-mind think tanks. These interconnected scholars - often secluded in great monasteries - pool their combined intellect to process complex magical theorems, solve ancient mysteries, and calculate the trajectory of Eukoria's future at a speed no single mortal mind could ever comprehend.

To follow Oclera is to believe that expanding the boundaries of mortal understanding is the highest divine calling. Their [[1. World Almanac/World/NPCs/Chosen Ones/index|Chosen Ones]] are often the "hands" of these think tanks, tasked with plunging into ancient ruins, recovering forgotten texts, and bringing new data back to feed the collective mind.

## [Divine Intercession](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=804)

Oclera rewards those who uncover great truths, educate the ignorant, and preserve history. They curse those who burn books, obscure facts, or hoard vital information for themselves.

\[Minor Boon::Oclera grants you a flash of perfect recall. Once per day, you can Recall Knowledge as a free action, and you gain a +1 status bonus to the check.]
\[Moderate Boon::The God of Knowledge physically manifests meaning into your mind. You permanently gain the effects of the Translate spell, allowing you to understand any text or spoken word.]
\[Major Boon::You gain absolute clarity of the universe's mechanics. The Recall Knowledge action never has the Secret trait for you, and you always know what degree of success you have achieved when using it. Once, when you would fail or critically fail a skill check to Recall Knowledge, you may choose to instead get a critical success. Once used, Oclera's focus shifts elsewhere, and this boon is permanently lost.]

\[Minor Curse::Your memory begins to slip as Oclera fogs your mind. You take a permanent -2 status penalty to all checks to Recall Knowledge.]
\[Moderate Curse::Written words rebel against your intellect. All text appears as illegible gibberish to you, rendering you permanently incapable of reading any language, including your own.]
\[Major Curse::Oclera completely erases a crucial piece of your consciousness. The GM secretly chooses one Intelligence-based or Wisdom-based skill. You automatically receive a Critical Fail when making Skill Checks with that Skill.]
