# Appearance

Garadwen possesses the timeless elegance befitting an elf who has outlived generations of her peers. Her vibrant auburn hair is meticulously woven into a complex, regal updo, crowned by a sweeping golden diadem that mimics the crashing waves of the [[God-Scar Strait]].

Her attire is a testament to Meridian wealth; she wears pristine outfits draped in silks and velvets, with a heavy cloak embroidered with elaborate gold filigree. Her posture is perpetually perfect, and her gaze is sharp, calculating, and completely unreadable.

# Personality

Garadwen embodies the very highest tenets of Lethos; she truly believes that the law is merely a cage for those who cannot read between its bars. Her demeanour is cool, patient, and ruthlessly polite. Because she is an ancient elf who has lived through apocalyptic divine conflict, she views the geopolitical struggles of Eukoria with a detached, chess-master's perspective.

Her primary motivation is the preservation and expansion of Meridia's absolute monopoly over global trade. She harbours a deep, secret disdain for brute force, viewing it as the tool of the unimaginative. Her greatest secret is that despite her public enforcement of orthodox magic, she meticulously studies the volatile tactics of the Dismagic pirates to the north - not out of sympathy, but to perfectly engineer their legal and physical annihilation.

# Relationships

- **The Board of the [[Concordat]] (Allies/Rivals):** Garadwen commands the respect and fear of the merchant-princes. They are her allies in wealth, but she knows any of them would unseat her if they found a legally binding loophole to do so.
- **The Church of [[Lethos, God of Trickery|Lethos]] & [[Aureon, God of Commerce and Industry|Aureon]] (Allies):** She is a favoured daughter of the Lethian church, utilizing their statecraft and sanctioned deception. She works closely with the high priests of Aureon, God of Commerce and Industry, to secure her iron-clad contracts.
- **The Hardliners of [[Solon, God of Law|Solon]] (Enemies):** The orthodox followers of Solon, God of Law, utterly despise her. They view her administration as a mockery of the spirit of the law, though they are completely unable to legally prosecute her.
- **The Pirates of [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Heretic's Wake/index|Heretic's Wake]] (Enemies):** She views the Dismagic fleets not just as a threat to her profit margins, but as a dangerous, volatile stain on the perfect order of her reality.

# History & Lore

Garadwen is an long-lived elf, and the only surviving member of the original merchant pact that eventually formed into the [[1. World Almanac/World/Locations/Eukoria/States/Meridia/index|Meridia]] that exists today. Today, she acts as its leader at the head of the Board of the [[Concordat]], but not without the support and aid of a large council of Oligarchs.

# Stats & Equipment

Garadwen is a Level 18 Sorcerer who almost never engages in direct, physical combat. A fight is merely a negotiation that has broken down, and she is rarely caught without her elite Silver Fox bodyguards. In an encounter, she utilizes extreme control and enchantment magic, relying on spells like suggestion and true target to manipulate the battlefield.
